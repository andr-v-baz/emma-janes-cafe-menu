Our little coffee hut ♡

Good food, fresh air and colourful garden tables.

Emma Jane’s is a small countryside coffee hut made for slow mornings, sunny afternoons and homemade food. Here, guests can enjoy fresh coffee, toasties, pancakes, sweet treats and peaceful outdoor seating surrounded by flowers and garden views. It’s a family-friendly and pet-friendly place, perfect for relaxed visits with children and pets.
